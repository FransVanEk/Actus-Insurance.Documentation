# ACTUS Insurance Documentation

Welcome to the ACTUS Insurance documentation. This section covers the application of ACTUS standards to insurance contracts, actuarial modeling, and insurance-specific regulatory requirements.

## Insurance Applications

The ACTUS framework extends to insurance products through specialized contract types and modeling approaches designed for the insurance industry.

### Supported Insurance Products
- **[Life Insurance Contracts](life-insurance)**: Term life, whole life, and universal life policies
- **[Annuity Products](annuity-products)**: Immediate and deferred annuities, variable annuities
- **[Property & Casualty](property-casualty)**: Auto, home, and commercial insurance contracts
- **[Reinsurance Agreements](reinsurance)**: Proportional and non-proportional reinsurance

## Key Insurance Features

### Actuarial Modeling
- **Mortality Tables**: Integration with standard and custom mortality assumptions
- **Morbidity Models**: Disability and health insurance claim modeling
- **Lapse Rates**: Policyholder behavior modeling and surrender patterns
- **Reserve Calculations**: Technical reserves and regulatory capital requirements

### Risk Management
- **Insurance Risk**: Underwriting risk, catastrophe modeling, and claims development
- **Market Risk**: Interest rate risk for long-duration liabilities
- **Credit Risk**: Counterparty risk in reinsurance and investment portfolios
- **Operational Risk**: Process risk and regulatory compliance risk

### Regulatory Compliance
- **Solvency II**: European insurance regulatory framework
- **IFRS 17**: International accounting standards for insurance contracts
- **GAAP**: Generally Accepted Accounting Principles for insurance
- **Risk-Based Capital (RBC)**: U.S. insurance regulatory capital requirements

## Insurance Contract Types

### Life Insurance
- **Term Life**: Temporary life insurance with level or decreasing benefits
- **Whole Life**: Permanent life insurance with cash value accumulation
- **Universal Life**: Flexible premium life insurance with investment options
- **Variable Life**: Life insurance with policyholder-directed investments

### Annuities
- **Immediate Annuities**: Income payments beginning immediately
- **Deferred Annuities**: Accumulation phase followed by income payments
- **Variable Annuities**: Investment options with minimum guarantees
- **Fixed Annuities**: Guaranteed interest rates and income payments

### Property & Casualty
- **Auto Insurance**: Personal and commercial vehicle coverage
- **Homeowners Insurance**: Property and liability coverage for residences
- **Commercial Lines**: Business insurance including general liability and property
- **Specialty Lines**: Professional liability, cyber insurance, and other specialized coverage

## Professional Use Cases

### For Insurance Companies
- **Product Development**: Standardized modeling for new insurance products

### For Actuaries
- **Valuation Models**: Technical reserve and fair value calculations

### for Regulators
- **Supervisory Review**: Standardized data for regulatory oversight
- **Market Analysis**: Industry-wide risk and solvency monitoring  
- **Policy Development**: Data-driven regulatory policy creation
- **International Coordination**: Consistent global regulatory standards
