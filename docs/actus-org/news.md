# News

## 2024 ACTUS Conference

The ACTUS Foundation organized its annual conference. The conference featured presentations and panel discussions on the many ways that the ACTUS financial contract standards can contribute to solutions to challenges in the financial industry.

## DaDFiR3 Conference

The DaDFiR3 Conference brought together 50+ attendees to discuss the DaDFiR3 Project, ACTUS, and their potential impact on the financial industry. Dr. Willi Brammertz held a presentation in which he elaborated on how ACTUS is set to improve regulation and bring substantial benefits to the financial industry.

Speakers at the conference included Magdalena Boškić (Sygnum Bank), Dr. Willi Brammertz (ARIADNE and ACTUS Financial Research Foundation), Philippe Brügger (FINMA), Mark A. Greenslade (Casper Association), Francis Gross (European Central Bank), Moritz Karber (European Central Bank), Ralf Kubli (Casper Association), and Eva Selamlar-Leuthold (Swiss Financial Innovation Desk).

## Webinar: The New World of Finance — Marlowe

Dr. Willi Brammertz presented a webinar titled "The New World of Finance" covering ACTUS and the Marlowe smart contract language.

## ACTUS Competition

The ACTUS Financial Research Foundation organizes a competition for building prototype applications that use ACTUS. The challenge for competitors is to provide services or applications utilizing ACTUS to provide increased value for financial institutions and other users with critical reliance on financial contracts.

Examples of such services include programs that increase the ease of incorporation of ACTUS into existing systems, as well as applications enabling special types of analysis, such as risk analysis. Both commercial proprietary and open-source applications are welcome.
