# Membership

## The ACTUS Users Association

The ACTUS Users Association is a 501(c)(6) not-for-profit trade association organized under the laws of the State of Delaware and granted not-for-profit status by the United States Internal Revenue Service.

The ACTUS Users Association is primarily concerned with building up and further developing an active community and industry support. It develops, maintains, and releases the ACTUS Technical Specification and a reference implementation in Java through GitHub under the ACTUS Core License.

## Organizational Structure

Decision-making responsibility rests with the board of directors, relying on recommendations and significant input from the members of the Association.

## How to Apply

To become a member of the ACTUS Users Association, fill out the membership application form. The board of directors will carefully review your application and inform you of the acceptance decision within twenty (20) business days.
