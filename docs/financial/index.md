ACTUS Financial supports:
- **Basel III/IV**: Capital adequacy and liquidity requirements
- **IFRS 9**: Expected credit loss models and hedge accounting
- **CECL**: Current Expected Credit Losses standard
- **Comprehensive Capital Analysis and Review (CCAR)**
- **Dodd-Frank Act**: Regulatory reporting requirements
